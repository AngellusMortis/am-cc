tree farm.

ex:
"tree" will have the turtle discover any trees in a straight line and then start harvesting them on a loop
"tree false" will have the turtle harvest any configured trees on a loop

notes:
Turtle expects the following inventories:

* empty chest to dump items into
* chest with fuel inside
* chest with a sapling inside to plant

Inventories can either be placed directly again turtle or connected via a modem.
