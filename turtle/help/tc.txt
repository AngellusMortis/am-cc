wrapper around turtleCore

ex:
"tc empty" will have the turtle return to origin and empty inventory
"tc refuel 10" will have the turtle return to origin add at least 10 fuel
"tc dig" will have the turtle dig and move 1 block forward
"tc digUp 3" will have the turtle dig and move 3 blocks up
"tc digDown 5" will have the turtle dig and move 5 blocks down
