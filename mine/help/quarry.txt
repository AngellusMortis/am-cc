digs out a cubic shape. Turtle's location is the top right corner

ex:
"quarry" will dig out a 16x16 quarry directly in front of the turtle with a max depth of 100 blocks
"quarry true 4 16 8" will dig out a 16 blocks to the left, 8 blocks forward and 4 blocks (current + 3) down stating with the block in front of turtle
"quarry false 50 4 4" will dig out a a 4x4 quarry 50 block down without placing walls

notes:
block above the turtle is expected to be an inventory with fuel
block to the left of the turtle is expected to be a dump inventory
